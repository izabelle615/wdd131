Foundational Music Artists

Discover the artists who shaped today's music scene

Site Name

Foundational Music Artists
This name represents the key musicians who laid the foundation for modern music genres.

Site Purpose

The site provides in-depth profiles of influential artists from rock, jazz, and hip-hop. These artists will be shown on how they changed the world of modern music.

Scenarios

  • What artist defined Rock and Roll?
  • How did jazz become a staple in modern music?
  • What led to the rise of hip-hop as a mainstream genre?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #34344A (Deep Blue-Gray) - Used for the background and footer.
Accent Color: #80475E (Deep Rose) - Used for call-to-action buttons and important elements.
Soft Pink: #CC5A71 - For hover effects and highlights.
Muted Gold: #C89B7B - For headings and borders.
Gold: #FFD700 - For emphasis and key sections.

Typography

Primary Font: Merriweather Sans, sans-serif (body text)
Secondary Font: Bubblegum Sans, sans-serif (headings)

Wireframe

The mobile view will feature a single-column layout with scrolling sections for each artist and genre. The desktop version will have a multi-column grid with quick links to artists, genres, and sections about their cultural impact.
